How to prepare for Cloud Practitioner Exam?


Material Which I followed for exam:

1. Courses

I took course by Stephane Maarek on Udemy for cloud practioner exam.

This course covers almost 99% syllabus.

For 1% you can refer Whitepaper.

Its rare that you might find question which is not covered by Stephane's Course,

Anyway its always better to refer whitepaper.

This course is about 14hours.

Click here for Course by Stephane Maarek


2. Whitepapers

Its good to read whitepaper, it covers all services which will be asked in exam.

Just read definations and what they are used for.

Click here for Whitepaper


3. Practice tests

Its extreamly important to give practice tests,

Since it gives you a taste of what kind of questions you might expect in exam.

Give practice tests as many possible. Try to get 80%+, 90%+ would be great.

If you dont get 80+ please go through course again.

Practice tests helps you to differentiate between the services and how you why some services are used.

So after giving practice test spend time on reading question and answers. This is very important.


Practice Test Links:

  • Click here for practice tests by Stephane Maarek
  • Click here for practice tests by Neal Davis



So, How did I prepared for exam?

Firstly, I took like 15 days to complete the course by Stephane on Udemy.

I also Created AWS Account to do Hands on.

Then I registeered for exam, I gave practice test and my score was around 50%,

So prior 7 days to the exam I completed the course again in detail which took me 4 days along with Whitepaper.

Then next 3 days I gave practice tests and everytime I scored 80%+.

I also refered PPT from Stephane Maarek course in these 3 Days. And Now I was confident to write the exam.


How you should prepare for exam? If you ask me. Then I would say give 21 days for preparation:

In first 10 days complete course in detail with hands on.

In these 10 days you should completely underestand the course.

Then for next 2 Days Read Whitepaper.

Then give practice tests and check how much you have understood.

In next 5 days you should do course one more time in datail.

In Next 4 days give all practice tests and read answers after giving.

In these 4 days, daily go through the PPT of Stephane Maarek course completely.

Try to remeber Keywords, Bold words and sentences from PPT.


Note:

  • For Non-IT folks dont worry about how you will manage, I am from Mechanical Engineering background.
  • While I was preparing for exam I dint have any work other than preparing course.
  • So if you have any project work you can add another 10 days for preparing, just to be on safer side.
  • Those who have done any AWS courses before can take this exam in one or two weeks time
